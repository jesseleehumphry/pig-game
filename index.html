RULES

STANDARD GAME

  • Players take turns rolling dice.
  • If a player rolls a 1 on either die:
    • The player loses their pocket
    • It's the next player's turn
  • If the player rolls and the same die rolls a 6 twice in a row:
    • The player loses their pocket & bank
    • It's the next player's turn

WIN CONDITION

STANDARD GAME

Players can win if their bank exceeds the win score.
